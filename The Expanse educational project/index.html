THE EXPANSE SERIES

Leviathan Wakes

"The Expanse" series begins with "Leviathan Wakes," a science fiction novel set in a future where humanity has colonized the solar system. The book follows two main characters, Jim Holden and Detective Miller, as they uncover a conspiracy that threatens to escalate into an interplanetary war. Along the way, they encounter a diverse cast of characters and confront powerful forces that are trying to keep the truth hidden. "Leviathan Wakes" is a thrilling space opera that combines elements of hard science fiction with action and political intrigue, and has been praised for its complex characters, world-building, and fast-paced plot.

Caliban's War

"Caliban's War" is the second book in "The Expanse" series, continuing the thrilling science fiction story of Jim Holden and his crew as they navigate a complex political landscape. A new character, scientist Praxidike Meng, is searching for his missing daughter and soon joins forces with Holden to uncover a conspiracy that threatens humanity. With complex characters, a richly detailed world, and a fast-paced plot, "Caliban's War" is a gripping sequel that expands the scope of the series.

Abaddon's Gate

"Abaddon's Gate" is the third book in "The Expanse" series, continuing the gripping science fiction story set in a future where humanity has colonized the solar system. In this installment, a mysterious gate opens up in space, leading to a vast array of new worlds and potential resources. But as the conflict between Earth, Mars, and the Belt intensifies, it becomes clear that the gate may also be a threat, with powerful forces seeking to control it for their own purposes. The novel follows the crew of the Rocinante, along with new characters, as they navigate this dangerous new frontier and confront new challenges and enemies. With its intricate world-building, complex characters, and gripping plot, "Abaddon's Gate" is a worthy addition to the series.
